  1. Success Criteria and Measurable Outcomes

    (a) Success Criteria
    • Have trained staff able to work with elderly.
    • Two staff trained in first aid.
    • Have our own facility.
    • Keep elderly safe.
    • Ensure elderly have food and tea.
    • Provide transport for elderly to and from home.
    • Find people trained to teach sewing, crochet, and knitting.
    (b) Measurable Outcomes

    We started with 18 elderly participants; currently we have 60, engaging in weekly activities including sewing, knitting, gardening, netball, choir, walking, and more.
