About Us

  1. Project Description

    (a) Statement of problem to be addressed

    Common conditions in older age include hearing loss, cataracts, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, diabetes, and more, which require proper care and support.

    (b) Goals and Objectives

    The goal of this project is to promote good health and prevent sickness while providing care:

    • Maintain the health and well-being of the elderly
    • Help them care for minor health problems and long-term conditions
    • Ensure recovery from illnesses and hospitalisation
    (c) Target Population

    Our target population is elderly individuals aged 60 years and above, including those with conditions such as Alzheimerâ€™s disease or advanced chronic illnesses.

    (d) Project Activities

    Activities for elderly include:

    • Exercise classes
    • Joining the elderly choir
    • Sports clubs for older adults (e.g., soccer)
    • Walking, jogging, or biking with peers
    • Gardening and planting vegetables
    • Sewing, crochet, and knitting
    • Playing Morabaraba or draughts
    (e) Key Staff
    • Organiser/Project Coordinator: Runs day-to-day activities, events, and trips.
    • Administrator: Maintains office services, attendance registers, and timesheets.
    • Financial Manager/Bookkeeper: Manages accounts, invoices, and bank transactions.
    • First Aid Person: Provides first aid as required by safety regulations.
    • Facilitators: Monitor elderly medication and support those who cannot attend programs.
    • Driver: Collects and drops elderly participants from their homes.
    • Cookers: Prepare meals for elderly during programs and events.
    • Cleaner: Cleans facilities, including floors and dishes.
    • Gardener: Maintains gardens and outdoor areas.

  2. Organization Background

    Masibambisane for Old Aged Citizens was established in August 2023 as a Non-Profit Company (NPC) with the certificate from the Department of Social Development.

    Zanele Nkosi, founder and chairlady, was inspired to start this organization to provide support and care for elderly people who otherwise lack access to resources and attention.

    The organization focuses on seniors aged 60 and above, providing exercise, social activities, educational workshops, and meals.

  3. Current Programs and Activities

    Programs run Monday to Thursday, focusing on physical and mental health. Activities include sewing, exercise, choir practice, and social gatherings.

  4. Goals & Objectives

    Goal: Keep elderly physically active and reduce risks of heart attacks and diabetes.

    Objective: Provide social support through programs and assist with professional care when needed.

    Program timelines and milestones will be updated as the organization grows and expands activities.
